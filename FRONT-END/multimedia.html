Varanasi:   Is the title of S.S. Rajamouli's highly anticipated, upcoming globetrotting action-adventure film, starring Mahesh Babu, Priyanka Chopra Jonas, and Prithviraj Sukumaran. The movie, previously known as the tentative title 'Globetrotter', was officially unveiled at a spectacular event in Hyderabad on November 15, 2025. Chinnadana Nee Kosam (transl. A little donation for you) is a 2014 Telugu-language romantic comedy film directed by A. Karunakaran and starring Nithiin and Mishti in her Telugu debut. The movie was produced by Nithiin's father and sister under the Sresht Movies banner. The plot revolves around Nithiin, a carefree young man who falls in love at first sight with Nandini. His family supports his pursuit, but Nandini initially rejects him. The film's title was inspired by a popular song from Nithiin's earlier hit, Ishq.

3.RCB-150.89 billion & Income per Match : ₹ 150 CRORE 😎

The Royal Challengers Bengaluru (RCB) is a professional Twenty20 franchise cricket team based in Bengaluru, Karnataka, which competes in the Indian Premier League (IPL). Founded in 2008 by United Spirits, the team plays its home matches at the M. Chinnaswamy Stadium. Known for its passionate and loyal fanbase, often referred to as the "12th Man Army," RCB has historically featured global cricket superstars such as Virat Kohli, AB de Villiers, and Chris Gayle. Despite reaching the IPL final on three occasions (2009, 2011, and 2016) and having a star-studded lineup for many years, the franchise had earned a reputation as "underachievers" for its inability to secure the coveted IPL trophy until recently. The team's women's counterpart, also owned by United Spirits, won the Women's Premier League (WPL) title in 2024, marking the franchise's first-ever title across both leagues. 

Despite reaching the IPL final on three previous occasions (2009, 2011, and 2016) and facing years of heartbreak, the team's long wait for a title finally ended in 2025.
 Under the captaincy of Rajat Patidar and coaching of Andy Flower, the Royal Challengers Bengaluru secured their maiden IPL title by defeating the Punjab Kings in a thrilling final.
 This historic victory, after 18 years, was widely celebrated
 and marked a significant milestone for the franchise and its stalwart player, Virat Kohli, who has been the only player to represent a single IPL franchise since the league's inception.
 The franchise also won the Women's Premier League (WPL) in 2024, bringing the first trophy to the overall franchise.
